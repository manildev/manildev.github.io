About me

  • I am currently working as a Researcher at the Bell Labs of Nokia. Prior to this I was a Research Associate in the Electronic Systems group at the department of Electrical Engineering in Eindhoven University of Technology. My research areas are confined to the different aspects of real-time embedded systems such as application-specific processors, novel memory devices & memory subsystems, system-on-chip architectures, interconnects, and embedded software.
  • I received my PhD in Electrical Engineering titled "Scalable and Bandwidth-Efficient Memory Subsystem Design for Real-Time Systems" from Eindhoven University of Technology, The Netherlands in 2015, Masters in Electrical Engineering with specialization in System-on-Chip design from Linköping University, Sweden in 2010, and Bachelor of Technology in Applied Electronics from College of Engineering Trivandrum , India in 2002. I have over four years of work experience in embedded software design and development for power management, device drivers and schedulers for multimedia and cyber-physical systems.
